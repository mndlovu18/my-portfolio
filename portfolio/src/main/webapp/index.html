About

My name is Minolta Ndlovu, a sophomore at Bryn Mawr College studying Computer Science and Economics. I am a front-end development enthusiast constantly seeking out new learning opportunities. My passion lies in Computer Science education with a focus on getting more women coders. On campus I am the president of Girls Who Code which is an organization that teaches women how to code. I am also a Codepath tech fellow. Codepath is an organization that brings technology classes on campus. Currently, I facilitated Codepath's Android development course.

During my spare time, I love capturing moments through photography and playing basketball
